Departments

  • Mathematics:

    • The Department of Mathematics at I.B. (PG) College, Panipat is one of the oldest and most esteemed departments in the institution. It offers robust undergraduate (B.A./B.Sc.) and postgraduate (M.Sc. Mathematics) programs, consistently focusing on applied computational techniques and strong academic foundations.

    HOD Of Department.

    Its HOD is Mrs. Arpana Garg.

  • Physics:

    • The Physics Department at I.B. (PG) College, Panipat, is a premier academic department offering comprehensive B.Sc. (Non-Medical) and B.Sc. (Computer Science) programs. Affiliated with Kurukshetra University (KUK), it provides high-quality theoretical and practical education through well-equipped laboratories and modern teaching tools.

    HOD Of Department.

    Its HOD is Mrs. Sonia.

  • Chemistry:

    • The Chemistry Department at I.B. (PG) College, Panipat, is a well-established science department that offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses. It provides hands-on practical learning, field visits, and hosts various academic events like the Science Conclave and chemical association activities.

    HOD Of Department.

    Its HOD is Mr. Vikram Kumar.

  • Computer Science:

    • The Computer Science Department at I.B. (PG) College, Panipat offers undergraduate programs including B.Sc. in Computer Science and B.C.A. Affiliated with Kurukshetra University, the department provides modern, air-conditioned computer labs with uninterrupted power backup and internet access.

    HOD Of Department.

    Its HOD is Mr. Vikram Kumar.
