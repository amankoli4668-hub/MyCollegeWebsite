IB (PG) College, Panipat.

About My College.

I.B. (PG) College, located on G.T. Road in Panipat, Haryana, is a premier, co-educational, government-aided institution affiliated with Kurukshetra University. Founded in 1956, the college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Arts, Science, Commerce, IT, and Management. It is known for its affordable education and modern campus facilities.

History of My College.

Established in 1956, I.B. (PG) College in Panipat, Haryana, is a prestigious co-educational institution . Originally founded as a women's college, it transitioned to co-education in 1966. Affiliated with Kurukshetra University, it has grown into a leading regional educational hub offering diverse undergraduate and postgraduate programs.
