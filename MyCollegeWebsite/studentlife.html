Student Life in IB (PG) college, Panipat.

Life at I.B. (PG) College, located on G.T. Road in Panipat, balances rigorous academics with a vibrant, active campus culture. Affiliated with Kurukshetra University, the premier co-educational institution offers robust extracurriculars, including NCC, NSS, and frequent industrial visits, fostering a supportive and engaging environment for students.

  • Campus Infrastructure:

    • The campus is fully CCTV-enabled with RO drinking water, Wi-Fi, and a central library offering a Book Bank, OPAC access, and N-LIST (UGC INFLIBNET) facilities for e-resources.

  • Extracurriculars:

    • Students frequently participate in activities hosted by the Student Council, Skill Development & Incubation Centre, and various departmental associations.

  • Sports & Fitness:

    • The college features dedicated gymnasia for both girls and boys, alongside expansive grounds for physical education.

  • Academics & Support:

    • The institution is organized into several blocks (including a new Science-cum-Administrative block) and provides regular remedial coaching, placement drives, and career counseling sessions.

  • Important Guidelines:

    • Students can avail themselves of bus pass facilities and are advised to keep a close watch on university exam dates and scholarship applications via the official board.
